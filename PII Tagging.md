## Advantages of using meta tags

Meta tagging PII has a number of benefits, including:

- _Streamlining the processes for data management and governance_
- _Assisting with adherence to data protection laws_
- _Providing specific protection measures to improve data security_
- _Enhancing data accuracy and quality through clear categorization_

Guidelines for PII meta-tagging

- _Create a comprehensive and uniform tagging taxonomy_
- _Make sure that all of the data elements have proper tags_
- _Review tags frequently, and make necessary updates_
- _Employee education on the value of meta tagging is necessary_
