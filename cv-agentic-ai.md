# H — Team Lead & Full-Stack Engineer

## Ethira — Team Lead, Signals Intelligence

As a team lead at Ethira, a GRC and compliance SaaS platform, Hugo has been part of designing and building agentic AI systems that serve customers in production. He has been responsible for driving improvements to the recommendation engine such as minimising hallucinations, improving answer precision, and increasing explainability of AI-generated compliance assessments. He has also focused on helping the team adopt AI tooling into everyday development, identifying where it creates real leverage and ensuring successful adoption.

The systems are built on LangChain and LangGraph with OpenAI, using RAG with embeddings and vector stores for document retrieval, cosine similarity for knowledge base matching, and Bayesian risk modelling. LLM fallback chains and circuit breakers ensure reliability in production. The platform runs on NestJS, PostgreSQL, Redis, and BullMQ, with a React 19 frontend.

**Stack**: TypeScript, NestJS, React 19, PostgreSQL, Redis, BullMQ, LangChain, LangGraph, OpenAI (Embeddings, Vector Stores, Assistants API), Docker, Sentry, Playwright
