APPROACH

solution ensured GDPR compliance and facilitated a comprehensive 360
-
degree customer 
view. Leveraging Unity Catalog, we implemented centralised data governance for controlled 
access to anonymised PII. This approach resolved manual data management challenges and 

accelerated business growth through improved customer insights and advertising strategies. 
Collaborating closely with stakeholders and internal data engineers, we tailored the solution to 
meet their specific needs. Furthermore, we provided training and support for seamless 
integration into their existing data landscape.
