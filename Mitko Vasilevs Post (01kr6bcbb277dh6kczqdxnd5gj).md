Anthropic just dropped Petri. A full automated AI red team in a box: generates audit scenarios, interrogates your model across multi-turn conversations, simulates tools, rolls back chats, and scores whether your LLM is deceptive, sycophantic, or dangerously helpful.


Most labs treat alignment tooling like nuclear codes. Anthropic built it, handed the repo to Meridian Labs, and bounced.


Anyone can fork. Anyone can audit. Anyone can check if their model is a sociopath.


Trust beats control.


Make sure you own your AI. AI in the cloud is not aligned with you; it’s aligned with the company that owns it.
