# Interactions




**Data Insights & KPIs**  

- What performance-related insights should the new platform enable, and what data is needed to power them?
    - **Driving model performance** - this is very complex to measure (what makes good driving) and we have several internal teams working on measuring this. The data will be in DataBricks and will include things like speed over/under speed limit, lane centering, interventions per km etc. Internally we refer to this as “Performance based on intervention and non-intervention data (KPIs for both utility/flow and safety related)”
- What should the platform make possible in terms of software release management insights, and what data will be required?
    - Details still to be shaped but overall our driving model artifacts are shipped to OEMs, trained on their data, validated and tested. We need to be able to track what models they have trained, what software SDK it can be bundled with and how it was validated to be safe on the road.
- More broadly — what business intelligence insights would you ideally like to surface, and what data underpins those?
    - Two main concepts: how do our ML model performance improve over time as we onboard new data and how is a single model compare with another model. The first mainly refers to leadership being able to prove that more data improves our model performance across different operational domains (countries, road types etc). The latter is more to enable deep insights into one or a handful of trained models to understand if they reach the capabilities required for deployment on the wider fleet / road.
