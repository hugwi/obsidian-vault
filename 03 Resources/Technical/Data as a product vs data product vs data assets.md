
What is your perspective on the differentiation between data assets and data as product?
Have been a topic we’ve discussed much at MediaMarkt tbh, but just from reading up on it Data as a product I interpret to be rather focused about the “treatment” of data as a product; address data quality issues. See https://martinfowler.com/articles/data-mesh-principles.html#DataAsAProduct, https://towardsdatascience.com/data-as-a-product-vs-data-products-what-are-the-differences-b43ddbb0f12 
Reading these articles they also distinguish “data as a product” being how to treat the data and “data products” seeing data as a way to “facilitate an end goal through the use of data”, so for example a dashboard or recommended restaurant nearby. 
To me it sounds more like a “data asset” is something used mainly internally and is a prerequisite for a data product.
