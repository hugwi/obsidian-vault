### In written form
## Context

CH is the stakeholder at the Growth Forecasting Tool AVA project, where I joined as a Data Engineer. I was tasked with creating the integration between Snowflake and our operational database in Amazon S3. During my time in the project I have worked closely with Carl as he is the main driver for the initiative. 

CH  recognized my drive, energy, and willingness to push the project forward. He appreciated the technical initiative I have brought.

## Communication & Documentation

My communication has been effective according to Carl. He felt that my status updates and documentation have been clear and well-structured, giving him good visibility into ongoing challenges. Carl mentioned that he generally feels well-informed about problems as they arise and values the transparency I've maintained throughout the process. Carl also highlighted how I can become even better at guiding and challenging stakeholders to find better solutions, showing that I'm navigating these relationships with confidence.

## Areas for Development

The main development area Carl identified centers on how I have approached blockers. While my communication about issues is good, there is room to be more creative and proactive in unblocking myself. Carl suggested that I could push harder to find alternative paths forward rather than waiting for blockers to resolve themselves. This includes being bolder in demanding more from stakeholders when needed and coming to conversations with more concrete solution proposals, not just problem statements. Carl mentioned that he had hoped to see more progress in the process at this stage. While he appreciates the work quality, finding shortcuts and creative workarounds where possible would help maintain project momentum.

## Looking Forward

I need to take full ownership of the Snowflake integration and drive it to completion before the engagement ends. This means continuing the strong communication and stakeholder management I've already established, while stepping up the proactive problem-solving and being more assertive in removing obstacles that stand in the way for progress.
