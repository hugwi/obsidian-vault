Mediamarkt has recently undergone significant growth in the data domain, leading to a consequential surge in challenges related to unregulated data management. In response to these critical concerns, an imperative data governance initiative was launched, guided by a set of fundamental objectives:

- Enhancing the discoverability of data assets
- Streamlining data access
- Establishing a robust framework for data accountability
- Promoting a culture of data literacy across the organization
- Ensuring rigorous compliance with data regulations, particularly the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

As part of this initiative, a multidisciplinary team was assigned the pivotal task of implementing a comprehensive data catalog, encompassing metadata records for an extensive repository of over 80,000 data tables, spanning both cloud-native and on-premises environments. Additionally, to meet GDPR requirements, the team assumed responsibility for developing and deploying a customized Personal Identifiable Information (PII) scanning solution, meticulously designed for the precise detection of PII within the dataset. In parallel, access policies pertaining to PII data were formulated, ensuring the highest standards of security and compliance in the handling of sensitive information.
