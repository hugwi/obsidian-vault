## Questions 

### 1. Context & Priorities 

* How would you describe Sensio’s biggest efficiency and workflow challenges in your daily operations?
* Which areas (Product & Technology, Customer Support, etc.) are currently most impacted by manual work or bottlenecks?
* What does success with AI adoption look like for you in the next 3–6 months?

### 2. Current State & Usage (5 minutes)

* What AI tools or solutions are already in use—and where are they working well, or falling short?
* Are there any specific workflow steps or processes where experimentation with AI has already shown promise or become a pain point?

### 3. Bottlenecks & Barriers (5 minutes) - to be investigated during an assessment 

* What prevents AI solutions from being integrated into daily work today (e.g. technical, organizational, cultural barriers)?
* Are there concerns around data security, governance, or the handling of sensitive information that have impacted broader adoption?
* Where do you experience the most friction or duplication across teams and markets?
* Are there any resistence from employees to adopt AI? 

### 5. Ways of Working & Ownership (5 minutes)

* What would ideal “ways of working” with AI look like for your teams? Who would own and drive these changes?
* Are there existing best practices or lessons learned internally you would want to build on—or pitfalls to avoid?
* What governance or roles (leadership, champions) are missing or needed to ensure clear ownership and progress?

### 6. Closing & Next Steps (5 minutes)

* Based on this conversation, how should we structure the next steps to best address Sensio’s needs?
* Are there other stakeholders or teams we should involve early in the assessment process?
* What would make this engagement valuable for you right from the start?
