We are continuously working on our EVP (Employer Value Proposition). This means that to make sure that our offer is strong, we are all year around - every year - collecting and reviewing market data as well as gathering qualitative feedback on our whole EVP. Our EVP includes everything at Netlight, from salary and vacation days to personal development, brilliant colleagues, our office, health benefits, and all other parts of our offer. Updates are reviewed and implemented at least twice per year.


The EVP work has been harder than ever this year, the market is volatile and hard to predict. This makes it difficult to know where we are heading and what the effect and consequences of different alternatives will be. We have therefore been working intensely during the whole year to make sure we act proactively and responsibly. Rise to Challenge is not over, but what we do see is a positive sales trend in Stockholm.


From a salary perspective, we have focused our work during spring on predictions of level updates for January 2024. In parallel, we have carefully monitored the situation. Given the positive trend in Stockholm, we now see an opportunity to implement two important updates of the consultant levels to be in effect already from July 1st 2023.


* **Salary increases derive from two sources**: **Level Alignment** (over time accounting for the more significant changes) and **Structural Consultant Level Updates**. 


	+ Structural Consultant Level updates effective July 2023: 
	
	
		- **Consultant**: Adding 2 000 base salary and 2 000 variable salary.
		- **Senior Consultant**: Adding 1 000 base salary.
* **Leveraged Share Program**: Managers and Senior Managers can accumulate extra index points, which they can utilize in the first program they enter when new on the level, if they want.
* **Parental Offe**r: We have developed our monthly parental leave meetups to a more Netlight-customized setup as well as created better tools for mentors who have mentees on parental leave.
* **Learning & Development** (L&D): Record-high number of 32 on-site Genuine Consulting Program courses, transformation in support to mentors, and increased support of AVAs, ES, and TS.
* **Bicycle benefit**: Netlight via Benify is now offering to benefit bicycles, meaning you can lease a bicycle through a gross salary deduction.


When it comes to salary, it is important to take two parts into consideration - that our consultant levels are at the right level, and that a career progression at Netlight enables a strong and market-competitive salary advancement.


Netlight consultants have multiple opportunities each year to calibrate our salaries. The major part of our salary increases consists of level alignment calibrations, and individual salary increases which reflect our career progression.


During recent months we have received many questions regarding inflation as well as the current global macroeconomic situation, and how does this affect our salaries?


The short-term answer is that neither inflation nor the global macroeconomic situation directly affects our salaries. Our salaries are based on the market, and *our target is that our salaries should be high considering comparable market salaries*. This is, for example, why Netlight salaries have increased even when inflation was low. However, from a long-term perspective, we expect that both inflation and global macroeconomics will affect the market. An uncertain market has often led to a slow development of market salaries, at the same time as inflation, historically, has led to an increase in market salaries. We see only limited effects so far, which have been confirmed through our external partner for salary statistics. In addition, we are, as described below, following the development closely through extensive data collection and analysis and are prepared to adjust accordingly.

Every Spring and Autumn, we review our EVP reviews and EVP updates. During the past years, we have had great access to EVP-related data, as we more systematically collect quantitative and qualitative data internally and externally. The data relevant to this decision come from recruits, resignations and a recurring survey, and qualitative interviews, with current and former Netlighters. This data is the base of the outcome of this update, combined with how we want to position ourselves compared to the market in terms of salary (market-high).


As the Leveraged Share Program is a biannual program, sometimes a Netlighter will level to Manager or Senior Manager untimely to a program. As the Leveraged Share Program is an important part of the monetary offer to our most senior Netlighters, we want to make the timing of the leveling matter less.


We can now proudly announce that **Managers and Senior Managers** **can accumulate extra index points**, which they can utilize in the first program they enter when new on the level, if they want.


This is a global update and more detailed information will be shared in a separate forum.


Operations CAP (Career Alignment Process) is done annually during Spring. We conduct reviews of our offering to ensure appropriate compensation level. To achieve this, Operations consistently engages the expertise of external service providers to procure market data, facilitating the benchmark of compensation and benefits. By consistently reviewing the compensation levels with external providers on a yearly basis, Operations acknowledges our colleagues' contributions. This approach, supported by external market data, enables us to make informed decisions that sustain our market position and contribute to the overall success of Operations.


This year we acknowledge the importance of being cautious and proactive with regard to our "Rise to Challenge" approach. Rather than implementing structural changes to the compensation levels, we analyse the market data, taking into consideration its unique business circumstances, financial stability, and long-term sustainability. This approach allows for tailored adjustments on an individual basis.


Since 2019, we have each month had meetups at the office with Netlighters on Parental leave, along with their children, with the purpose of staying in contact with Netlight and colleagues in the same situation, and to receive individualized support from coworkers and Netlight both during the parental leave and upon return to work, regarding combining profession with parenthood.


During spring 2023, we have developed these meetups into an even more Netlight-customized setup, using our internal knowledge and network. In addition, we have created better tools for mentors to support their mentees in the re-boarding, as well as more contact points with for example the sales team.


During spring 2023, the Learning and Development team has been a great part of developing and supporting Netlighters, not least with a record-high number of 32 on-site Genuine Consulting Program courses as well as growing the team with one person. There has also been a transformation in the support of mentors – from the previous Mentor Boot Camp to a new improved Mentor Boost Camp, with the purpose of giving better tools for mentors in supporting their mentees.


In addition, to create a better, smarter and closer organization, the learning and development team have to a greater extent been supporting colleagues such as available consultants, ES and TS, as well as mentors.


The Netlight Benefits portal is your personal portal that gathers everything related to your benefits and the Netlight employment offer. It makes it easy for you to browse your latest benefits, get a full overview and select the benefits that matter to you.


There are several interesting news this year, for example, starting today, Netlight via Benify is now offering benefit bicycles! This means that you can lease a bicycle, both electrical and non-electrical through a gross salary deduction which is adjusted monthly from your salary payment. Since the deduction is made from gross salary, it results in a tax relief effect (direct translation from "skattelättnadseffekt") and thus a more beneficial type of partial payment. You can read more on Benify's website: [http://benefits.netlight.com](http://benefits.netlight.com/).


Just to give a brief reminder, as we do updates several times every year, some of the changes we did effective January 2023 was to add 1 vacation day, rotate 1 000 SEK variable salary to base salary, and added an interval of 2 000 SEK more variable salary to the Associate Consultant level. In addition, we added 1 000 SEK to the Senior Consultant and Associate Manager levels. We also introduced a new senior level at operations, called Operations Manager. This level is now part of the LSP and essential as part of the executive structure. Lastly, we employed two more trainers to Learning & Development (L&D) and made GoClimate an active climate investment for all Netlighters. [Read more here](https://netlight365beta.sharepoint.com/sites/EVPUpdates/SitePages/EVP-Update-January-2023.aspx).


During the Fall of 2023, we will again evaluate our offer including the Consultant levels, and adjust where applicable. And of course, follow closely what happens within and within the market. Besides these areas, we will keep working on keeping the EVP strong for Netlighters within all categories.


For Manager, Senior Manager, Partner, and Principal level the salary and the leveraged share program will be summarized as a total monetary offer. The leveraged share program is a qualitative incentive with a first outcome during 2024. During the end of 2023, we will assess the program and salary levels together.


In every EVP update, there are many contributors outside of the EVP group. We often create or take help from focus groups specialized/deep diving in one or many certain areas - thank you for all the help. And of course, thank you to everyone involved in the EVP survey!
