Instrumental for Journey 2 are different roles that unlock gradually, from deliver coaching to interviewing candidates to being a mentor. The roles builds upon each other and help expanding your relationship network as well as deepening your understanding of Netlight. To become a mentor is a huge milestone, commitment, and responsibility at Netlight. Therefore, you need experience, perspective and to reflect on the mentoring role before deciding why and if this is the next step in your Journey. Make sure to share and discuss the reflection with your mentor.

# Mentorship:
- Why is mentoring the right next step for you? There are different ways to support others in their careers (int 2, DC, Buddy etc).
- Have you been a mentor/coach before? Outside of Netlight? What did you learn that can be of value in a mentor role at Netlight?
- What feedback have you received regarding your qualities and experiences that can be useful in the mentor role? Who have you discussed mentoring with.
	- Per Andersson
